About ImageTooling

Revolutionizing image editing with cutting-edge AI technology since 2025

Our Story

Founded in 2025, ImageTooling began as a small startup with a big vision: to make professional-quality image editing accessible to everyone. Our team of AI researchers and software engineers developed proprietary algorithms that outperform traditional background removal techniques.

What started as a simple background removal tool has grown into a comprehensive image editing platform serving millions of users worldwide, from individual creators to Fortune 500 companies.

Our Technology

At the heart of ImageTooling is our deep learning technology that has been trained on millions of images to understand and separate subjects from backgrounds with pixel-perfect precision.

Unlike traditional computer vision techniques, our AI models continuously learn and improve, delivering better results with each update. We've pioneered several innovations in semantic segmentation that are now industry standards.

Our Mission

We believe everyone should have access to professional-quality image editing tools, regardless of their technical skills or budget. Our mission is to democratize visual content creation by making complex editing tasks simple, fast, and affordable.
