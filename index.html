Frequently Asked Questions

Find styles to common questions about our service

What types of images work best for background removal?

Our AI works best with high-contrast images where the subject is clearly distinguishable from the background. Photos with solid color backgrounds, well-lit subjects, and minimal overlapping elements yield the best results.

How do you handle complex elements like hair or fur?

Our advanced AI algorithms are specially trained to detect fine details like individual strands of hair or fur. For best results, ensure good lighting and contrast between the subject and background.

What file formats do you support?

We support all common image formats including JPG, PNG, WEBP, and GIF. Output is always delivered as PNG with transparent background for maximum quality.

Is there a limit to image size or resolution?

Free accounts can process images up to 0.5MP (about 800x600px). Paid plans support up to 12MP (about 4000x3000px). For higher resolutions, contact our enterprise team.

How secure is my uploaded content?

All uploads are encrypted in transit and at rest. Processed images are automatically deleted from our servers after 24 hours. We never use your images for training our AI models.

Can I batch process multiple images at once?

Yes! Our Professional plan and above support batch processing. You can upload up to 50 images simultaneously depending on your subscription level.

What makes ImageTooling different from other background removers?

Our proprietary AI delivers superior edge detection, especially for challenging cases like hair, fur, and transparent objects. We also offer more post-processing options and faster processing times than competitors.

Do you offer API access for developers?

Yes! Our Business and Enterprise plans include API access with comprehensive documentation and client libraries for popular programming languages.
